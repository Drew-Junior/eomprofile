Founder, Academic, & Edtech Entrepreneur

Dr. Enoch Opare Mintah

Visionary Leader · Author · Global Innovator

Dr Enoch Opare Mintah (PhD, FHEA, CMBE, MRes, MBA, MSc, BA) is a global higher and professional education consultant and Edtech entrepreneur. He holds faculty and visiting lecturer positions in the UK, China, India and some African countries, teaching sustainability-related courses to support green skills development and just transition.

He has founded educational and media platforms, including Study Sustainability Hub (SSH), Sustainability News Africa (SNA), Sustainability Skills and Careers Launchpad Africa (SSCLA) and Leaps and Heights Foundation.

He is the Sustainability Lead for Leaps and Heights Services Limited, UK, a consulting company that delivers ESG training and advisory services for listed companies and MSMEs on the African continent.

Enoch is a published author and is committed to bridging the global green expertise gap through skills capacity development, innovation and policy engagement.
